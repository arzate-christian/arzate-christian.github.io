Research interests

At the Honda Research Institute JP (HRI JP), I'm working on human-robot interaction through artificial intelligence methods. My collaborators at the HRI JP and I design human-computer interaction methods to enhance the relationship between humans and social robots.

We are always looking for eager interns to come to collaborate with us. If you are interested, send me an email to christian.arzate@jp.honda-ri.com.
